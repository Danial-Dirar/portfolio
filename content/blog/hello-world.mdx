Welcome to my corner of the internet.

I'm Danial — a computer-science engineer from Dhaka. I spend most of my time on
three things:

- **Machine learning** — time-series forecasting, model benchmarking, research
  pipelines that hold up under scrutiny.
- **Generative AI** — image and video pipelines that run on hardware I control.
- **Local AI for business** — private assistants and RAG systems that never send
  a company's data to someone else's cloud.

This blog is where I write down what I learn while building. Expect build logs,
honest benchmarks, and the occasional strong opinion about running AI on your
own GPU.
